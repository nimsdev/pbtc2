How It works

Getting started is pretty easy!

Let's Go!

1. Think Of Something

Chances are you already have this step completed. If so, keep scrolling. If not, brainstorm things you'd like to see on the web!

2. Send Me An Email

You can click here to bring up my contact form, or scroll to the bottom of this page. In the 'message' box, let me know what you want. Please be as detailed as possible. If you already have a mockup, designs, or examples of what you want in hand, upload the images using this site. Your email will be sent to jon@jonathanbelok.com.

3. Chatting

I will respond to you within a day after receiving your email. I'll ask a question or two depending on the content of your email.

During this time, we'll send e few emails back and forth detailing the site you want. After that, I will come up with a mockup and a quote.

Before I start the site, we can meet in person and go over the minor details before I start working on your site.

4. Sit Back & Relax

That's it! Your work is D-O-N-E! The rest is up to me. Your site will be up and running in no time!
